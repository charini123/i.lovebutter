About us

I Love Butter, a warm, cozy café nestled right in the heart of Mosman. Here, the aroma of freshly baked, buttery treats fills the air, making every visit feel like coming home.

At I Love Butter, we believe in the little moments that make life sweeter—whether it's your morning ritual, a catch-up with friends, or simply taking a quiet moment for yourself. Our team pours love into everything we do, and nothing brings us more joy than sharing that with you, one bite at a time

Welcome to our café, where every treat is made with care, and every visit feels like a homecoming.
